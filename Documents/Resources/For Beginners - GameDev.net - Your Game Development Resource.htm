The methodologies used in making games are closest to iterative, prototype, and ad hoc. However, software development in the video game industry is far from mature, and though most games are developed using similar methodologies, there is no universal methodology, and certainly nothing that has been formalized to any significant level of detail.

Also, making a game involves much more than just software development. Art, game design, business and marketing, and other aspects heavily influence how a game is made.
